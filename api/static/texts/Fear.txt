There are a few things you can try to change the mood of a Fear cat:

1)Providing plenty of opportunities for play and exercise.
2)Spending quality time with your cat.
3)Providing a scratching post and other items for them to interact with.
4)Engage your cat in interactive playtime to help distract.
5)give them some space.

If you are unable to change the mood of your cat despite trying these methods, it may be best to consult with a veterinarian or a professional cat behaviorist for additional help.
It's important to keep in mind that each cat is unique and what works for one may not work for another. It's also important to be patient and not to use physical punishment or harsh punishmet.
